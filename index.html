About

I’m a full-stack internet engineering generalist and entrepreneur with 23 years of professional experience building web applications. I’ve worked extensively at both small-scale startups (through two startup acquisitions) and at large-scale web properties, including Craigslist and Yahoo!. I also have substantial experience in mobile applications and in leading teams.

I’m cofounder and CTO of StoriumEdu, a web-based, real-time, collaborative storytelling game that turns students into motivated writers. I work out of my home in Sedona, AZ, and hike daily in this stunning desert landscape.

I’m available for remote short- and long-term contract work. Please get in touch if you’d like to discuss working together.
